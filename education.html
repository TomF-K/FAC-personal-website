Education

I love talking about music almost as much as I enjoy playing it, and I'm passionate about giving people the tools they need to be musically independent.

Since 2015 I've been running Free Bass Transcriptions, which offers free sheet music and lessons for bass players, along with an accompanying YouTube channel:

One of the upshots of the COVID-19 pandemic was that it forced me to start teaching online, which has allowed me to help people all over the world get closer to their musical goals.
